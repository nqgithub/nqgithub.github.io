THE CLOUD

Every moment in this life is important and should be saved and shared. However, what happens when your local computer runs out of space? If you have an in-home fire and your photos are destroyed, or you just simply misplace your photos, then what? Well thankfully you can sign in or create a free cloud-based Capture account that gives you access to upload and view your photos at any time. Also, with new Capture certified cameras and built in Wi-Fi and NFC technology upload your photos directly to your computer. No cords, no hassle. Now just simply click and transfer without stress or delay. New users get 10 gb of free cloud space. Plus order a Capture certified camera now and receive 5 gb free of cloud storage. Start making and saving your memories, memories that will last a life time.
